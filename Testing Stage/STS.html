"Roguelike Card Game I Never Knew I Wanted"



Slay the Spire is a card game that is known for it's Roguelike aspect due to it being a deckbuilder that involves adding cards to your deck, hopefully finding a combination of these cards to form a coherent deck. What makes Slay the Spire different from other card games is the fact that there are numerous possibilities in both deck-building but also challenges that player can encounter as they climb up the spire to defeat bosses and the root of the spire.


A deckbuilder card game might seem confusing and intimidating at first glance, however as you continue to play the game, you will get a glimpse of the possible combinations that make the cards in your deck work together. Additionally, there are relics that can be obtained through random events, or the fearsome elite that could make or break your run through their challenging combats. After the player gets through multiple floors of the spire, they will be met with 1 of 3 bosses in each act, acting as a gatekeep and whether the player is ready to take on the next act.


The player has a choice to play and choose between 4 characters, namely the Ironclad, the Silent, the Defect, and the Watcher.

The Ironclad is a character built with survivability and using exhaust and hp as their primay sources of damage and utility.

The Silent on the other hand is an agile character that uses shivs, poison, and discard to make quick plays that could quickly dispose of enemies.

The Defect is a unique character that makes use of orbs which could either be a lightning orb (damaging random enemies), frost orb (providing block), dark orb (stacking damage that could be evoked), and a plasma orb (proving extra energy).

Lastly, the Watcher which utilizes various stances like Wrath stance, doubling the damage she deals and takes, with Calm Stance being the contrasting stance, and Divinity that triples damage dealt while giving extra energy.



Slay the Spire as a card game is an interesting take on the Roguelike genre, in a way that gives players a wide variety of choices while maintaing replayability through out the game. It is a card game that challenges the player in different situations while giving the player certain options that could work or not depending on how the player plays the game. It is a puzzle game that helps the player solve itself, giving us enterntainment along the way.
