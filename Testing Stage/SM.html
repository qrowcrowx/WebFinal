An Old Time Favorite classic, Super Mario Bros. (NES) is a video-game made for Nintendo Entertainment System in year 1985. It is highly praised for having accurate controls and is regarded as one of the best video games of all time as well as one of the best selling games of all time


Groundbreaking gameplay elements were introduced in Super Mario Bros., setting the bar for future platformers. Precision movement and leaps are possible because to the responsive and tight controls. The level layout was thoughtfully planned, providing a satisfying blend of difficult obstacles, well-kept mysteries, and rewarding power-ups. Every level in the game is fun to play because of the game's simple and pleasant fundamentals, such leaping on adversaries or smashing blocks.


What makes Super Mario Bros. still special up to this day is that of its nostalgia, capturing the hearts of players for decade up until now, The game has vibrant visuals and catchy music which makes the game more memorable and immersive in a fun way. It also offers a good level of replayability and encourages the player to explore every level, and every level is carefully designed with hidden paths/shortcuts, warp zones and secret power-ups to boost your in game experience making it easier to play with.


The game is not perfect however since it also has few problems which some players might find frustrating. NES SUper Mario Bros. has no saving mechanism meaning that once you die and your hp had run out, you will start from the beginning (hence why it's replayability is great) however, it can be inconvenient and time-consuming since you will have to finish the game in one sitting. The gameplay is nice but it mainly focuses on platforming and same concept every level, you reach the end of the map and proceed to another. It might become too reptitive since its gameplay is limited however this may only be applicable to some people who appreciate much more content in a game, this concept that Super Mario Bros. NES have is actually its charm and what makes the game special and groundbreaking during its time.



In conclusion, SUper Mario Bros. NEWS remains a timeless classic cwhich defined the platforming genre during its years and made its way to one of the best, iconic, nostalgic and charming game of all time. The game does lack a saving mechanism and it being limited to its gameplay (which consist of platforming mostly) may considered to be downside depending on your overall appeal on what a game should be for you. Regardless, if you're an old-timer who had played this game during its peak, it surely is worth your while to revisit your childhood experience as an adult.
