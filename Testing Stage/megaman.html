"I AM MORE THAN A ROBOT WILLY!"



Mega Man X4, originally called Rockman X4 in japan is an Action-Platform game developed by Capcom. It is the Fourth game released in the Mega Man series. The game offers a mixed of platforming and challenging boss with its iconic run and gun gameplay.


A new intriguing twist is added by allowing players to choose between two playable characters: Mega Man and Zero. Nothing much have changed with Mega Man X's gameplay style, everything is complete exactly the same as he was before including his iconic charge shot while Zero, a new character to play brings a fresh experience with his close-quarters combat abilities and sword attacks. The dual-character system addes depth and replay value for the game, having two characters have their won storyline and abilities made it much more interesting.


A break from the simpler plots of earlier games in the series, Mega Man X4 stands out for its compelling narrative. The constant war between humans and machines is explored in the game, along with the internal conflicts the lead protagonists experience. The game explores topics like loyalty, identity, and the morality of technological progress, giving the intense gameplay emotional weight.



Mega Man X4's character growth is deserving of appreciation. Dialogue and sequences that are well-written give Mega Man X and Zero more character and complexity. New characters that are vital to the story's development are also introduced in the game, including the enigmatic Colonel and the enigmatic Iris. Players will become emotionally invested in the outcomes of these people, elevating the overall experience above straightforward run-and-gun combat.


Mega Man X4's vivid, bright graphics and intricate character designs serve as a showcase for the PlayStation. The settings are varied, including frigid vistas, mechanical strongholds, futuristic cities, and more. Each has its own distinctive aesthetic flare. The well-animated character sprites and creative boss designs make for visually stimulating confrontations. The game's soundtrack is also deserving of appreciation because it harmonizes flawlessly with the action-packed gameplay. Aside from the addition to Zero and bosses added, gameplay-wise there's not much had been added and therefore can be a bit repetitive if you had played other megaman game before. regardless the game is definitely still worth to try since it offers new character, amazing visuals and a well written story.
