"I HONOR THOSE WHO'VE RISEN, NOT THOSE WHO'VE FALLEN"



Apex Legends, game developed by Respawn Entertainment to compete with battle royale-hero shooter games, Around 2019 Battle Royale games have become popular and with EA's Apex Legends, it bursts into scene garnering more players than they expected gaining a dedicated fan base not only to indulge in the unique gameplay, but also within the story's visual cinematic and lore.


Unlike other battle royale games, Apex Legends provides an exciting and frantic gameplay experience. Every match feels fast and exciting because of the game's movement mechanics, which were influenced by Titanfall and allow for fluid and acrobatic gameplay Players are kept interested and alert throughout the entire game by the combination of quick mobility, responsive controls, and thrilling gunplay. What also sets the game apart from battle royale is that the characters (referred to as legends) have unique skills that are used depending on the situation.


Although it may seem like apex might be a perfect game to play, it also has downside to share such as its steep learning curve. While playing Apex Legends offers a rewarding and fun experience it might be a bit stressful for newcomers since you will be forced to learn a lot of Legends abilities, weapons the whole map in-game which is like 4 and are all mostly massive. The matchmaking might also be unfair sometimes since you might be in a much lower rank but the matchmaking might force you to play with higher rank players. Since Apex legends is a team-based game, it can be less enjoyable if you're always solo playing with random teammates can sometimes lead to frustrating experience especially most of them won't be communicating or be looking for teamwork to win. lastly, in-game purchases are massive and can be addictive so make sure you pay attention and be sure that whatever you are spending is worth the money.



Overall, Apex Legends is definitely worth to try especially if you're into fast-action battle-royale games with unique character and abilities that focuses on teamwork. The game remains enjoyable even for casual and competitive games although the competitive part of the game might be much harder if you play the game solo so what we would advise is to play the game casually and enjoy the character and maps until you get used to them. Only then we can recommond a player to play ranked games and be sure to communicate properly with your team to make your chances of winning much further.
