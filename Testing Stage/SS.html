Hollow Knight: Silksong is the epic sequel to Hollow Knight, the award winning action-adventure. As the lethal hunter Hornet, journey to all-new lands, discover new powers, battle vast hordes of bugs and beasts and uncover ancient secrets tied to your nature and your past.

Hollow Knight: Silksong doesn't currently have a release date. There were hopes Silksong would release in the first half of 2023: when it appeared at the Xbox & Bethesda showcase in June 2022, Xbox made a point of saying it was focused on games coming within the next 12 months, with the Xbox Twitter account confirming that window soon after.

However, despite that commitment from Xbox, we apparently shouldn't expect to see Silksong until the back half of the year at the earliest. Matthew Griffin, who does PR work for Team Cherry, announced on Twitter in May 2023 that Silksong wouldn't make the June 2023 deadline, saying "it's gotten quite big, so we want to take the time to make the game as good as we can."

Hopefully, we'll still see Silksong in 2023, but we won't know until an official release date is announced.
