COUNTER STRIKE 2: A NEW ERA

Valve unveiled Counter-Strike 2, which uses the Source 2 game engine, on March 22, 2023. It is presently scheduled for release as a free update to Global Offensive in Summer 2023.

The Next Era of Counter-Strike has started selecting few people for limited testing, Valve had announced a video on their improved Maps and new Gameplay Trailer at March 22,2023. Counter-Strike 2 is confirmed to be released at summer 2023. During the testing period, Valve will be evaluating their new implementations in-game as well as user feedback with their new upcoming version of Counter-Strike. Other features will be implemented once the game is fully polished, when it is officially launched this summer of 2023.

Fans have long been waiting and anticipating a new version Counter-Strike, Counter Strike Global Offensive (CSGO) did change Counter-Strike forever but it wasn't until a new competitor for Counter-Strike was made, Valorant which was made by Riot Games then shook the tables which caused some CSGO Professional players had switched to Valorant. The playerbase dropped as players then switched to valorant due to the reason that Valorant has more frequent update than CSGO as well it looked more modern.

Finally, 'Touchstone' maps, such as the original Dust 2, are used to showcase some of the visual differences between CSGO and CS2, and have gotten a modest upgrade in terms of some effects and textures, but otherwise remain untouched. In Counter-Strike 2, community map developers will have access to all of the Source 2 tools and rendering features.
